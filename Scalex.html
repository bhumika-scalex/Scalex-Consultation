After 18 years in enterprise staffing — managing recruitment and account relationships for Fortune 500 companies, global financial institutions, and leading technology brands — the answer to what mid-size companies actually need from a recruitment partner became clear.

Not a large agency where their mandate is one of hundreds. Not a junior recruiter learning on their time. A specialist who has sat in those rooms, managed those escalations, and built the networks that actually produce the right hire. Someone who treats every mandate with the rigour of a retained executive search — regardless of level.

Bhumika held senior roles at Adecco, Indeed, and leading staffing organisations, where she led large-scale staffing engagements, ran 60+ enterprise MSP accounts with full P&L ownership, and built the relationships and discipline that Scalex Consulting draws on for every search today.

Our Process

End-to-End Recruitment Workflow

From signed mandate to Day 1 joining confirmation — we own every step so you do not have to manage the process alongside your own work.

01
Day 0

Intake & Briefing

30-minute mandate call. Role scope, team context, expectations, timeline, and what has failed before. Signed agreement before sourcing begins — no exceptions. We leave knowing the role better than most internal TA teams.

02
Days 1–7

Active Search

Passive network outreach, Boolean LinkedIn search, Naukri Resdex, and referral chains. We reach the senior candidate who is not applying anywhere. Target: 15–20 raw profiles per mandate.

03
Days 2–8

Candidate Screening

Structured 30-minute call per candidate. Technical fit, motivation to move, salary expectation, notice period, and competing offers — all confirmed before a profile is submitted to you.

04
Days 7–10

Shortlist Delivery

3–5 profiles with written commentary per candidate — why this person, key strengths, notice period, and any risk flags. Not just CVs. Every profile submitted is a recommendation, not a possibility.

05
Days 10–21

Interview Coordination

We own scheduling, candidate prep before every round, and feedback collected within 24 hours of each interview. Friday search updates to every active client — without being asked.

06
Days 18–45

Offer to Joining

Offer bridged on both sides. Counter-offer handled before it becomes a resignation. Weekly candidate check-ins through notice. Day 1 joining confirmed. 30-day free replacement guarantee in writing.
